Where Desert Winds
Carry Ancient Melodies

Gunsar Folk Music is a cultural organization dedicated to the preservation and promotion of the rich folk music traditions of Rajasthan. Born in the dunes of Barmer and Jaisalmer, our music tells stories of love, valor, devotion, and the eternal bond between humans and nature.

From the soulful notes of the Sarangi to the rhythmic beats of the Dholak, every performance is a journey through centuries of oral tradition — vibrant, living, and deeply moving.

Who We Are

Our Story

Born in the dunes, carried by desert winds, heard across the world.

The Gunsar Legacy

Founded in 1998 in the village of Gunsar, Barmer district, our organization was born from a simple but powerful belief: that the folk music of Rajasthan is not just entertainment — it is a living archive of our people's soul, history, and spirit.

The name "Gunsar" means "essence of qualities" in the local dialect — and that is precisely what we seek to capture and share. Over the past 25 years, we have grown from a small community gathering into an internationally recognized cultural institution.

We work directly with master musicians from the Manganiyar, Langa, and Bhopa communities, ensuring that their traditions receive the respect, documentation, and global platform they deserve.

Our Mission

We believe every folk melody carries a story — of monsoon rains on desert sand, of campfires under starlit skies, of love found and lost across endless dunes.

Our mission is threefold: Preserve by documenting and archiving traditional compositions; Promote by bringing folk music to national and international audiences; and Pass On by educating the next generation in these ancient arts.

Through concerts, workshops, recordings, and digital outreach, we ensure that the music of Rajasthan's desert communities continues to resonate for centuries to come.

What We Offer

Our Programs

🎤

Live Concerts

Full-scale performances featuring our ensemble of 15–30 artists. We perform at cultural festivals, royal palaces, heritage hotels, and international stages, bringing the complete spectacle of Rajasthani folk to life.

Performances
🎓

Music Workshops

Hands-on learning sessions where participants can learn to play traditional instruments like Dholak, Khartal, Morchang, and Algoza under the guidance of master folk musicians from Rajasthan.

Education
🏕️

Desert Evenings

Intimate folk music evenings in the desert, held at sunset or under stars. These exclusive experiences are curated for small groups, offering an unforgettable connection with the Thar landscape and its music.

Experience
📚

Cultural Documentation

We actively document rare folk compositions, oral histories, and musical traditions through audio-visual recordings in collaboration with cultural institutions and universities worldwide.

Heritage
🌍

International Tours

Gunsar Folk Music has toured over 30 countries, performing at prestigious venues in Europe, Japan, North America, and the Middle East — always carrying the authentic spirit of Rajasthan.

Global
👶

Youth Training Program

A dedicated program for young musicians aged 10–25, teaching traditional instruments, folk compositions, and the cultural context of Rajasthani music to ensure generational continuity.
